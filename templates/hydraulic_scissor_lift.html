🔧 Hydraulic Scissor Lift

This project uses hydraulic principles to build a functional scissor lift platform, ideal for demonstrating Pascal’s Law in action.

✅ Key Features

  • Based on Pascal's Law
  • Lifts light to moderate loads
  • Constructed using syringes or mini pistons
  • No electrical power required
  • Compact and portable design

Hydraulic Scissor Lift - Conceptual Illustration

⚙️ Working Mechanism

When pressure is applied to the fluid inside the connected syringes, the force is transmitted equally in all directions. This force causes the lift arms to rise, lifting the platform smoothly.

🔧 Wind-Powered Mobile Charger

An eco-friendly mobile charger that generates electricity using a small wind turbine to charge mobile devices.

✅ Key Features

  • Harnesses renewable wind energy
  • Portable and off-grid solution
  • Low-cost and efficient
  • Good for remote areas
  • Charges small electronics (5V USB output)

Wind-Powered Mobile Charger – Conceptual Illustration

⚙️ Working Mechanism

The wind rotates a small turbine connected to a DC generator. The generated voltage is then regulated and fed into a USB output for mobile charging.

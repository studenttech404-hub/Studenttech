🔧 Pedal-Powered Water Pump

A manually powered water pump system driven using a bicycle mechanism, suitable for rural and emergency water pumping applications.

✅ Key Features

  • No fuel or electricity required
  • Simple bicycle-based power transfer
  • Low maintenance
  • Environmentally friendly
  • Useful in off-grid areas

Pedal-Powered Water Pump – Conceptual Illustration

⚙️ Working Mechanism

When the user pedals the system, rotational energy is transferred to a crank and piston or centrifugal pump, drawing water from a source and pushing it through the outlet pipe.
